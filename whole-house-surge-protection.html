Protect Your Biggest Investments

Modern homes are filled with highly sensitive microprocessors. It isn't just your computers and TVs anymore—your refrigerator, washer/dryer, HVAC system, and LED lighting all rely on delicate circuitry. A single power surge from a lightning strike or a utility grid spike can instantly destroy thousands of dollars worth of equipment.

Why Power Strips Aren't Enough

Standard plug-in surge protectors (power strips) only defend the items plugged directly into them, and their protective components degrade over time without you knowing. Eminent Power Solutions installs Type 1 and Type 2 Surge Protective Devices (SPDs) directly into your main electrical panel.

  • Total Shielding: An SPD blocks transient surges before they ever enter your home's wiring network, protecting hardwired appliances like ovens, EV chargers, and AC units that can't be plugged into a power strip.
  • Internal Surges: Up to 80% of all power surges actually originate inside the home when large motors (like an AC compressor or vacuum) cycle on and off. Panel-mounted SPDs neutralize these internal spikes, prolonging the life of your smaller electronics.
  • NEC Mandated: The National Electrical Code (NEC) now requires whole-home surge protection for all new dwelling services and panel replacements. We ensure your home is fully up to modern code.

Surge Protection FAQs

No consumer surge protector can withstand a 100% direct lightning strike to your home's structure. However, it will aggressively defend against the incredibly common surges caused when lightning strikes a nearby power pole or transformer, sending massive voltage spikes down the utility lines into your home.

Most high-quality SPDs will last 3 to 5 years, depending on the frequency and severity of surges in your area. They act like a sponge, slowly absorbing excess voltage until they are "full." The units we install feature LED indicator lights so you can easily see when it's time for a replacement.
