Protect Your Biggest Investments

Modern homes are filled with sensitive microprocessors. It isn't just your computers and TVs anymore—your refrigerator, washer/dryer, HVAC system, and LED lighting all rely on delicate circuitry. A single power surge from a lightning strike or a utility grid spike can destroy thousands of dollars worth of equipment in an instant.

Why Power Strips Aren't Enough

Standard plug-in surge protectors (power strips) only defend the items plugged directly into them, and they degrade over time. Eminent Power Solutions installs Type 1 and Type 2 Surge Protective Devices (SPDs) directly into your main electrical panel.

  • Total Shielding: An SPD blocks transient surges before they ever enter your home's wiring network, protecting hardwired appliances like ovens and AC units.
  • Internal Surges: Up to 80% of power surges originate inside the home when large motors (like an AC compressor) cycle on and off. Panel-mounted SPDs neutralize these internal spikes.
  • NEC Mandated: The most recent updates to the National Electrical Code require whole-home surge protection for all new dwelling services and panel replacements. We ensure your home is fully compliant.
