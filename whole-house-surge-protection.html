Why Power Strips Aren't Enough

Standard plug-in surge protectors (power strips) only defend the items plugged directly into them, and their protective components degrade over time without you knowing. Eminent Power Solutions installs commercial-grade Surge Protective Devices (SPDs) directly into your main electrical panel.

The EPS Master-Level Process

  • Type 1 vs. Type 2 SPDs: Depending on your utility setup, we install either a Type 1 SPD (mounted at the meter to stop outside surges immediately) or a Type 2 SPD (mounted inside the breaker box to handle both internal and external spikes).
  • Cascading Protection: For ultimate defense, we design "cascaded" systems, layering a heavy-duty panel SPD with high-quality point-of-use strips to catch any residual electrical "noise."
  • NEC Code Mandates: The National Electrical Code (NEC 2020 update) now officially requires whole-home surge protection for all new dwelling services and panel replacements. We ensure your home is fully protected and up to modern standards.
