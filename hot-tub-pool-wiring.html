The Ultimate Standard in Electrical Safety

Installing a new hot tub, swim spa, or pool is an incredible upgrade to your MD or VA home's outdoor living space. However, wiring these systems is one of the most heavily regulated aspects of the National Electrical Code (NEC Article 680). A single improper connection can result in lethal shock hazards. Hover or tap below to see our safety standards.

GFCI Protection

We install dedicated 50 or 60-amp Ground Fault Circuit Interrupters that instantly cut power if a water fault is detected.

Safety Disconnects

Code dictates a manual emergency disconnect switch must be mounted within sight of the spa, but safely away from the water.

Equipotential Bonding

We build a copper bonding grid connecting all metal parts to equalize voltage and prevent stray currents from shocking swimmers.

Secure Trenching

We safely trench and bury PVC conduit from your home's main panel to the outdoor equipment to protect the wiring from weather.

Zero Margin For Error

Eminent Power Solutions does not cut corners on outdoor wiring. We handle all load calculations, 811 Miss Utility coordination, trenching, and complex bonding requirements to ensure your family and guests are 100% protected.

Code-Compliant Installation Process

  • Site Assessment: We calculate the required amperage of your specific spa unit against your home's existing electrical panel capacity.
  • Underground Routing: We dig the required 18-inch trenches to safely route PVC-protected wiring beneath your lawn or patio.
  • Component Wiring: We wire the disconnect box, the GFCI breakers, and the spa pack itself, ensuring water-tight seals on all outdoor connections.
  • County Permitting: Water features are highly scrutinized by county inspectors. We handle all permits and stand by our work to guarantee it passes inspection.
