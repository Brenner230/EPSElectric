The Ultimate Standard in Safety

Installing a new hot tub or pool is an incredible upgrade to your home. However, Hot Tub, Spa & Pool Wiring is one of the most heavily regulated aspects of the National Electrical Code. A single improper ground or bonding connection can result in a lethal shock hazard.

Pool & Spa Wiring FAQs

The National Electrical Code (NEC) dictates that the maintenance disconnect switch must be readily accessible and within sight of the hot tub, but it must be at least 5 feet away from the inside wall of the spa. This ensures someone cannot touch the switch while sitting in the water.
