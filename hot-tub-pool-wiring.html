The Ultimate Standard in Electrical Safety

Wiring pool systems is one of the most heavily regulated aspects of the National Electrical Code (Article 680). Improper connection or inadequate grounding can lead to lethal shock hazards. Eminent Power Solutions executes every installation with absolute precision to protect your family.

Our Code-Compliant Process

  • Dedicated GFCI Circuits: Most spas require a dedicated 50 or 60-amp circuit. We install specialized breakers to cut power instantly if a fault is detected.
  • Emergency Disconnects: Code requires a manual disconnect within sight of the spa. We mount these for both safety and aesthetics.
  • Equipotential Bonding: We connect all metal parts of the structure and pump to a bonding grid, preventing stray currents from shocking swimmers.
  • Underground Conduit: We safely trench and bury protected wiring from your panel to the outdoor equipment.
