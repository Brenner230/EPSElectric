Expanding Your Home's Power Grid

Adding a detached garage, a backyard workshop, a pool house, or an Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU) requires robust, reliable electricity. Running overhead wires is often unsightly and vulnerable to the DMV's high winds and falling branches. Eminent Power Solutions specializes in secure, underground electrical distribution.

Safety, Depth, and Code Compliance

Burying live electrical lines is highly regulated. A DIY mistake here can result in catastrophic damage or severe injury during future landscaping. Our master electricians handle everything by the book:

  • Direct Burial vs. PVC Conduit: We evaluate soil conditions and structural needs to determine whether direct burial UF-B cable or THWN wire in rigid PVC conduit is the safest, most durable solution.
  • Trenching Depth Codes: We execute trenches at proper NEC-mandated depths (typically 18 to 24 inches depending on the conduit used) to ensure total safety from surface disturbances.
  • Sub-Panel Installations: For heavy-duty workspaces, we run large-gauge feeder wires from your main home to a new, dedicated sub-panel in the outbuilding, giving you local control over your breakers.
