Expanding Your Home's Power Grid

Adding a detached garage, a backyard workshop, a pool house, or an Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU) requires robust, reliable electricity. Running overhead wires is often unsightly and incredibly vulnerable to the DMV's severe thunderstorms, ice, and falling branches. Eminent Power Solutions specializes in secure, underground electrical distribution.

Safety, Depth, and Code Compliance

Burying live electrical lines is highly regulated. A DIY mistake here can result in catastrophic damage, code violations, or severe injury during future landscaping. Our master electricians handle everything by the book:

  • Direct Burial vs. PVC Conduit: We evaluate soil conditions and structural needs to determine whether direct burial UF-B cable or heavy-duty THWN wire encased in rigid PVC conduit is the safest, most durable solution for your specific property.
  • Trenching Depth Codes: We execute trenches at proper NEC-mandated depths (typically 18 to 24 inches depending on the conduit and voltage used) to ensure total safety from surface disturbances and frost heaves.
  • Sub-Panel Installations: For heavy-duty workspaces or ADUs, we run large-gauge feeder wires from your main home to a new, dedicated sub-panel in the outbuilding, giving you local, safe control over your breakers.
