Safety, Depth, and Digging

Burying live electrical lines is highly regulated. A DIY mistake here can result in catastrophic damage, code violations, or severe injury. Our master electricians ensure every step of the excavation and wiring process is handled by the book.

The EPS Master-Level Process

  • Miss Utility Coordination: Before a shovel hits the dirt, we coordinate with 811 to have all existing underground gas, water, and telecom lines marked to prevent catastrophic damage.
  • Direct Burial vs. PVC Conduit: We evaluate soil conditions to determine whether direct burial UF-B cable or heavy-duty THWN wire encased in rigid PVC conduit is the safest, most durable solution for your specific property.
  • Heavy-Gauge Feeders: For outbuildings demanding high power (like welding shops or EV charging garages), we perform voltage drop calculations to ensure the buried feeder wires are thick enough to carry the current over long distances without performance loss.
