Capitol Hill, D.C. Historic Wiring

Historic Knob & Tube Remediation

The buyers of this beautiful 1890s rowhouse could not secure homeowner's insurance due to active knob-and-tube (K&T) wiring. Preserving the historic plaster ceilings was paramount. Using specialized endoscopic cameras, we strategically routed modern Romex cabling, abandoning the brittle K&T in place.

  • Circuit tracing and separation of historic vs. modern wiring.
  • Surgical wire fishing to preserve original plaster and lath.
  • Upgrading ungrounded 2-prong outlets to NEC-compliant GFCIs.

Columbia, MD Fire Prevention

Whole-Home Aluminum Wiring Remediation

Built in 1971, this home featured single-strand aluminum wiring, presenting a severe fire hazard. A complete tear-out was cost-prohibitive. We executed a CPSC-approved remediation by opening over 80 individual receptacles and switch boxes, permanently pigtailing copper wiring to the aluminum.

  • Systematic opening and inspection of all branch circuit devices.
  • Application of anti-oxidant compounds to prevent wire corrosion.
  • Precision-torqued AlumiConn® copper pigtail installations.
