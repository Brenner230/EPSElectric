Keep Your Real Estate Transaction on Track

Nothing stalls a home sale faster than a red-flagged electrical inspection. Whether you are a realtor trying to close a deal or a buyer negotiating repairs, you need an electrician who understands the urgency and strict demands of the National Electrical Code (NEC).

Common Inspection Failures We Fix:

  • Missing GFCI/AFCI Protection: Outlets near water (kitchens, bathrooms, outdoors) must have Ground Fault Circuit Interrupters. We can rapidly retrofit your home to meet current safety standards.
  • Double-Tapped Breakers: When two wires are jammed into a breaker designed for only one, it creates a serious fire hazard. We correct panel crowding safely.
  • Unpermitted DIY Wiring: "Handyman specials" like exposed wire splices outside of junction boxes, improper grounding, or reversed polarity are massive red flags for appraisers.
  • Outdated Panels: Identification of recalled Federal Pacific or Zinsco panels that must be swapped out before an insurance policy will bind.
