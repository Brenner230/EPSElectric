Missing GFCI/AFCI

Outlets near water sources must have Ground Fault protection. We rapidly retrofit kitchens, baths, and exteriors to meet modern safety codes.

Double-Tapped Breakers

When two wires are jammed into a breaker designed for one, it creates a massive fire hazard. We safely correct panel crowding.

Unpermitted DIY

"Handyman specials" like exposed wire splices, reverse polarity, and improper grounding are instant red flags for appraisers.

Recalled Panels

Insurance companies often refuse to bind policies on homes with Federal Pacific (FPE) or Zinsco panels. We replace them fast.

Reverse polarity means the hot and neutral wires were wired backward on the receptacle. While a lamp might still turn on, it creates a severe shock hazard because the exterior metal parts of appliances (like the shell of a toaster) can become electrified. We can quickly test and correct this.

It depends on the scope of work. Swapping out a few ungrounded outlets for GFCIs or fixing a double-tapped breaker usually does not require a permit.
