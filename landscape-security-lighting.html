The EPS Master-Level Process

  • Weatherproof Seals: Outdoor electrical work demands precision. We use silicone-sealed junction boxes and specialized outdoor-rated connectors to prevent moisture from causing shorts or tripping GFCIs.
  • Underground Conduit: For landscape lighting grids, we safely bury wiring to protect it from lawnmowers, edgers, and rodents.
  • Voltage Drop Calculations: Long runs of low-voltage wire across a large yard will lose power, causing the lights at the end to look dim. We calculate the exact wire gauge needed to keep every light shining at full brightness.
