Safety, Security, and Curb Appeal

A dark home is an inviting target. Professional exterior lighting serves a dual purpose: it acts as a primary deterrent against intrusion while dramatically elevating your home's nighttime aesthetics.

Our Exterior Lighting Services

  • Hardwired Security Floodlights: We hardwire high-lumen, motion-activated LEDs directly to your home's power, ensuring protection when you need it most.
  • Smart Camera Integration: Hardwiring for security systems like Ring or Nest to ensure zero exposed wires.
  • Low-Voltage Landscape Design: robust transformers to power beautiful pathway lights, architectural uplighting, and deck illumination.
  • Photocells & Timers: Set it and forget it. We wire lights to turn on automatically at dusk and off at dawn.
