Safety, Security, and Curb Appeal

A dark home is an inviting target. Professional exterior lighting serves a dual purpose: it acts as a primary deterrent against intrusion while dramatically elevating the nighttime aesthetics of your architecture and landscaping. Eminent Power Solutions specializes in weatherproof, long-lasting exterior electrical infrastructure.

Our Exterior Lighting Services

  • Hardwired Security Floodlights: Battery-powered lights fail when you need them most. We hardwire high-lumen, motion-activated LED floodlights directly to your home's power supply, ensuring continuous protection.
  • Smart Camera Integration: Seamless installation and hardwiring for modern security ecosystems like Ring Floodlight Cams or Nest systems, ensuring no exposed wires.
  • Low-Voltage Landscape Design: We install robust transformers to power beautiful, low-voltage pathway lights, architectural uplighting, and deck illumination to extend your outdoor living space.
  • Photocells & Automated Timers: Set it and forget it. We can wire your exterior lights to astronomical timers or photocells so they turn on automatically at dusk and off at dawn.
