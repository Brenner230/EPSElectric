Wake up to a full battery. We install high-speed, dedicated electric vehicle charging circuits for homes in Frederick, D.C. suburbs, and Northern Virginia.

Faster, Safer At-Home Charging

Relying on a standard 120-volt wall outlet (Level 1) can take days to fully charge a modern electric vehicle. For DMV commuters, a Level 2 Charging Station (240-volt) is essential, delivering up to 40 miles of range per hour of charge.

Eminent Power Solutions is experienced in hardwiring and outlet installations for all major EV brands, including Tesla Wall Connectors, ChargePoint, Rivian, and Ford Charge Station Pro.

The EPS Installation Process

  • Load Calculation: We assess your home's current electrical panel to ensure it can safely handle the sustained high amperage of an EV charger.
  • Dedicated Circuitry: We install a dedicated 50 or 60-amp circuit directly from your breaker box to your garage or driveway.
  • Code Compliance: We ensure proper wiring gauge and conduit installation to meet strict local Maryland and Virginia electrical codes, preventing fire hazards.
  • Heavy-Up Integration: If your older D.C. or Frederick home lacks the capacity, we can bundle your EV charger installation with a seamless panel heavy-up.
