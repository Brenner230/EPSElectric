The EPS Master-Level Process

  • Rigorous Load Calculation: We aggressively assess your home's current electrical panel to ensure it can safely handle the sustained high amperage (typically 40 to 60 amps) required by a Level 2 charger without stressing your system.
  • Code-Compliant Wiring: We use the exact proper wiring gauge and conduit installation to meet strict local Maryland and Virginia electrical codes, completely eliminating the serious fire hazards associated with DIY or sub-par handyman installs.
  • Heavy-Up Integration: If your older D.C. or Frederick home lacks the capacity, we can bundle your EV charger installation with a seamless panel heavy-up.
