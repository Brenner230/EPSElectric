Lighting is the Ultimate Upgrade

Nothing transforms the mood, utility, and perceived value of a space quite like professional lighting. Whether you are updating a 1980s home by removing outdated fluorescent boxes or installing a statement piece in a two-story foyer, Eminent Power Solutions delivers flawless aesthetic results without damaging your drywall.

Our Lighting Specialties

  • Recessed & Canless LED Lighting: We expertly map out and install modern, ultra-thin LED wafers that provide brilliant, even illumination while drastically reducing your energy footprint.
  • Chandelier & Heavy Fixture Mounting: Installing a heavy chandelier requires structural reinforcement. We install specialized fan and fixture bracing boxes to safely secure high-weight lighting in foyers and dining rooms.
  • Under-Cabinet & Accent Lighting: Precision low-voltage wiring to highlight kitchen backsplashes, architectural tray ceilings, and custom cabinetry.
  • Smart Dimmers & Controls: Upgrade your existing switches to smart dimmers (like Lutron Caséta) to control your home's ambiance directly from your phone.
