Respecting Your Home's Finishes

The mark of a true master electrician is leaving the home looking like the lights magically appeared there. We utilize specialized fiberglass rods, flexible drill bits, and endoscopic cameras to "fish" wires through finished ceilings and walls, keeping drywall patching to an absolute minimum.

The EPS Master-Level Process

  • Strategic Layout Design: We help you plan the layout, ensuring recessed lights are properly spaced to eliminate dark spots and avoid casting harsh shadows over kitchen prep areas.
  • Load Verification: While modern LEDs pull very little power, adding multiple fixtures to an old circuit can still cause issues. We verify circuit capacity before adding new loads.
  • Code-Compliant Support: We never hang heavy fixtures on standard plastic ceiling boxes. We always retrofit heavy-duty, fan-rated bracing systems into the ceiling joists for maximum safety.
