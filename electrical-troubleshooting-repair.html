Do Not Ignore the Warning Signs

Electrical problems do not fix themselves; they only degrade further. A continuously tripping breaker is doing its job by stopping a fire. If you smell ozone (burning plastic), hear buzzing from an outlet, or experience persistent flickering, cut power to the area and call an emergency master electrician immediately.
