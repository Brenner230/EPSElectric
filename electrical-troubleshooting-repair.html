Stop Guessing. Start Diagnosing.

Electrical problems are rarely as simple as they appear. A dead outlet in your bedroom might be caused by a loose neutral wire in the kitchen, or a failing breaker in the basement. Blindly replacing parts is dangerous and expensive. Eminent Power Solutions utilizes advanced diagnostic equipment to trace faults directly to their source.

Common Issues We Resolve:

  • Tripping Breakers: If resetting a breaker becomes a weekly chore, your circuit is either severely overloaded, or you have a dangerous short circuit hiding in your walls.
  • Flickering or Dimming Lights: Often a sign of a loose connection, an overloaded neutral wire, or a failing main service drop from the utility company.
  • Dead Outlets or Half-Power: When an entire room or wall goes dead, it usually indicates a broken continuous circuit, a tripped hidden GFCI, or a melted wire nut.
  • Buzzing Sounds or Burning Smells: This is a severe emergency. If you hear buzzing from a switch or smell burning plastic near an outlet, turn off the breaker immediately and call us.
