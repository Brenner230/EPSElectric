Seamless Power During Outages

Between severe summer thunderstorms, hurricane remnants, and harsh winter freezes, power outages in Maryland and Virginia are a reality. A whole-home standby generator ensures your HVAC, refrigeration, sump pumps, and medical equipment stay running without skipping a beat.

Master-Level Generator Wiring

Unlike portable generators that require manual setup and dangerous extension cords, a permanently installed standby system activates automatically seconds after the grid goes down. Eminent Power Solutions handles the complex electrical integration:

  • Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS): We wire the crucial ATS unit that safely disconnects your home from the utility grid and routes power from the generator, preventing dangerous "backfeeding."
  • Critical Load Panels: If you don't need the entire house powered, we can design a sub-panel that isolates and protects only your most critical circuits (fridge, well pump, furnace).
  • Load Management: Smart modules that ensure your generator doesn't stall by prioritizing where power goes first during high-demand moments.
