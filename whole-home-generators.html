Seamless Power During Outages

100% Automated

No going outside in the rain to pull cords. When the grid drops, your standby system transfers power to your house automatically within 10 seconds.

Endless Fuel Supply

Unlike portable units that require constant refueling with dangerous gasoline cans, standby generators tie directly into your home's natural gas or LP lines.

Clean, Safe Power

Standby units produce "clean" electricity with low harmonic distortion, which is required to safely run the delicate microprocessors in modern TVs and appliances.

Critical vs. Whole

We can wire your system to power the entire house, or create a sub-panel to power only your most critical circuits to save you money on a smaller unit.

Master-Level Generator Wiring

  • Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS): We meticulously wire the crucial ATS unit that safely disconnects your home from the utility grid and routes power from the generator. This prevents dangerous "backfeeding" that can electrocute utility workers fixing the lines.
  • Smart Load Management: We install smart shedding modules that ensure your generator doesn't stall by temporarily pausing high-demand appliances (like dual AC units) if they try to start at the exact same time.
  • Code Compliance: From ensuring proper clearance distances from windows to proper trenching for the electrical conduits, we handle all inspections to guarantee your unit is legal and safe.
