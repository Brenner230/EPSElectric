Seamless Power During Outages

Between severe summer thunderstorms, hurricane remnants, and harsh winter freezes, extended power outages in Maryland and Virginia are a reality. A whole-home standby generator ensures your HVAC, refrigeration, sump pumps, security systems, and critical medical equipment stay running without skipping a beat.

Master-Level Generator Wiring

Unlike portable generators that require manual setup in the rain and dangerous extension cords, a permanently installed standby system (from brands like Generac or Kohler) activates automatically seconds after the grid goes down. Eminent Power Solutions handles the complex electrical integration:

  • Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS): We wire the crucial ATS unit that safely disconnects your home from the utility grid and routes power from the generator, preventing dangerous "backfeeding" that can electrocute utility workers.
  • Critical Load Panels: If you don't need the entire house powered, we can design a sub-panel that isolates and protects only your most critical circuits (fridge, well pump, furnace, internet router) to save you money on a smaller generator.
  • Load Management: We install smart modules that ensure your generator doesn't stall by prioritizing where power goes first when high-demand appliances (like dual AC units) try to start at the same time.
