Ditch the batteries and exposed cords. We provide permanent, hardwired infrastructure for modern smart homes, security systems, and high-end audio/visual setups.

Reliability Built Into the Walls

True home automation shouldn't rely on charging batteries or tripping over extension cords. To get the most out of premium smart home technology, the devices need to be permanently integrated into your home's electrical grid. Eminent Power Solutions specializes in concealing the complexity, leaving you with seamless, architectural finishes.

Smart Ecosystem Integration

  • Hardwired Security: We pull dedicated wire to power Ring Floodlight Cams, Nest Doorbells, and exterior security arrays. Hardwiring ensures your cameras never go offline due to a dead battery during a crucial moment.
  • Smart Lighting Systems: Upgrading traditional switches to smart dimmers (such as Lutron Caséta or Leviton smart switches), requiring proper handling of neutral wires that older homes often lack.
  • Samsung Frame & Smart TVs: Flawless mounting of modern, ultra-thin televisions. We install recessed media boxes behind the screen to hide power outlets, HDMI cables, and soundbar wiring directly inside the drywall.
  • Smart Electrical Panels: Installation of next-generation load centers (like Span or Leviton Smart Load Centers) that allow you to monitor and control individual circuits from your smartphone—perfect for solar and EV integration.
