Working with Contractors & Homeowners

A successful renovation relies entirely on scheduling. Led by Master Electrician Eric Pumphrey, we coordinate tightly with general contractors, architects, and county inspectors to ensure rough-ins are completed on time so the drywallers and finishers are never kept waiting.

The EPS Remodeling Standard

  • Blueprint Review & Load Calculation: Before we pull a single wire, we evaluate your blueprints and appliance spec sheets to calculate if your existing electrical service can handle the new load.
  • Seamless Rough-Ins: We precisely measure and map out every switch, outlet, and recessed can light to perfectly align with your custom cabinetry and tile layouts.
  • Flawless Finish Work: Once the paint is dry, we return to install your luxury fixtures, smart switches, and faceplates with absolute care to ensure zero damage to your new walls.
