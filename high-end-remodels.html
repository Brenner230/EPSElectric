Flawless Execution for Luxury Spaces

A high-end remodel demands more than just basic wiring; it requires an eye for architectural detail and an understanding of modern electrical loads. Whether you are gutting a historic D.C. rowhouse or adding a luxury master suite in Frederick, Eminent Power Solutions ensures the infrastructure behind your walls is as premium as the finishes on the outside.

Our Remodeling Capabilities

  • Custom Kitchens: Modern kitchens require immense power. We install dedicated circuits for induction cooktops, double ovens, commercial-grade refrigerators, and custom under-cabinet LED arrays.
  • Spa Bathrooms: Safe, code-compliant wiring for high-draw luxuries like steam showers, radiant floor heating, heated towel racks, and dedicated GFCI circuits near water sources.
  • Home Additions: Seamlessly integrating new square footage into your existing electrical grid, including load calculations to determine if a panel heavy-up is required.
  • Smart Home Integration: Hardwiring for centralized lighting control systems, automated smart blinds, and concealed A/V networking.
