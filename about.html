Why Hire a Master Electrician?

In the electrical trade, titles matter. A Master Electrician is the highest designation achievable in the industry. It requires thousands of hours of documented field experience, rigorous background checks, and passing exhaustive state-level examinations regarding the National Electrical Code (NEC) and local municipal amendments.

Only a Master Electrician is legally authorized to design electrical systems, pull necessary municipal permits, and oversee the integrity of your home's infrastructure. Don't trust your property's safety to uncertified contractors.
